ABOUT US

We Provide World-Class Education And Training Facility

About Us

B.V Reddy School is one of the leading schools in chitoor with students from different parts of chittoor. Our focus is to imbibe qualities like empathy, consideration, earnest, generosity, being acceptable and so on. So teaching is done amidst nature, so as to nurture the innocent minds and help them understand and create a love for learning and to ignite a keen spirit of enquiry, imagination and the ability to question and to reason in the young minds.

To provide a secure and stimulating environment in which a continuous desire and excitement for further learning is fostered. To offer the highest possible standards of academic excellence thereby preparing a sound foundation on which the later stages of the children’s education and career can be successfully built.

Vision

Our vision at B.V. Reddy Sr. Sec. School is committed to serve the students by developing lifelong skills imbued with values and anchored on relevant basic curriculum through competent and committed teachers with adequate functional resources and thus empowering students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school-respect, tolerance and excellence, providing the best possible education for the student with a good firm foundation.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high quality education that delivers a transformative learning experience to all our students.

Engaging them in an effective teaching based on our core values and providing them the best up to date education practices which enable students of our school, to achieve their academic ambitions and lifetime careers. At the same time, we nurture their individual talents and emphasize on the importance of educating the whole child with attention to their physical, emotional and social development.

Our Motto

Our motto “Bhuddhiryasya Balam Thasya” means “Intelligence is strength “ We envisage an education that stems from a genuine curiosity and eagerness to explore and also develop the overall personality of the child.

We wish to enable all learners’ access to learn through the provision of:-

  • Innovative learning methodologies.
  • To dispose the pressure of conventional learning and helping them in all aspects of learning.
  • To prepare them to be able to relate with teachers confidently.
  • Concentrating on safety and security of the students.
  • Holistic development of the student through physical, emotional and spiritual discipline.
  • To develop positive attitude towards learning by sharing their thoughts.
  • To develop positive attitude towards learning by sharing their thoughts.
